Threads

A Thread is a complete flow that comprises at least one Trigger and one Event. Threads bring your automation ideas to life, executing complex tasks across multiple platforms.

Triggers

Triggers are the initial conditions that kickstart your threads. From email notifications to API calls, set your triggers to initiate the workflow you desire.

Events

Events are the tasks or actions that follow your triggers. They are the "That" in your "If This, Then That" equations, enabling you to execute specific actions in response to initial conditions.
